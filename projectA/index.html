Scientific Name:

[ Emotibact ]

Discovered by / at:

Jonathan Engalla, Febuary 1st, 2023 at approximately at 9:43 PM

Family:

At this point in time there is no related bacteria to the Emotibact. Due to its unique nature of only reacting to human emotion there are no known bacteria that have the same behavior

Habitat:

Macdonalds French Fries specifically from happy meals

Appearance:

The nuetral state of the Emotibact is an ellipse with circles sticking out from all four sides

Key Characteristics:

  • Reacts strongly towards anger, happiness, and sadness
  • Anything else besides emotions does not get a reaction.
  • Not only consumes emotions but embodies it as well
  • Can move at extremely fast paces
  • Can change its state from a semi-solid to completely liquid

What we know so far about Emotibact? The Emotibact is the only bacteria known that can react to human emotions. Its ability to percieve emotions and change its appearance and mannerisms can be seen by how it interacts with different tears that came from various emotions. For example when a tear of joy is placed the bacteria turns into a bright shade of yellow after consuming it. Once it absorbs the drop it start changing, its body moving so rapidly and changes so often it almost looks like a rainbow. All of these aspects will change depending on the kind of tear that is placed onto the dish. The strongest reactions we have seen so far are anger, sadness, and happiness. We will continue to test the diffferent emotions and record our findings
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Using the mouse or tear dropper you can drop tears on the rectangular plate. Once that is done just sit back and watch it work.
