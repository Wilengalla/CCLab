What is a Meme?

Memes are cultural symbols and ideas that spread virally through internet platforms, often in the form of images, videos, phrases, or concepts. They can be humorous, satirical, or poignant and typically evolve rapidly as they are shared, remixed, and adapted by internet users. Memes often reflect current events, pop culture references, or shared experiences, and they serve as a way for people to communicate ideas and emotions in a playful or relatable manner.
